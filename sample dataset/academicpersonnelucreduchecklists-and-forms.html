Checklists and Forms

The Academic Personnel Office has developed a series of checklists and forms to assist in assembling academic review files and to provide academic personnel information in a standardized format. For checklists and forms in PDF format, Adobe Acrobat Reader is required to read and print the forms. To download a copy go to the Adobe website: https://get.adobe.com/reader/. If you have questions or can’t find the information you need regarding Academic Compensation, please send an email to academicpersonnel@ucr.edu.
