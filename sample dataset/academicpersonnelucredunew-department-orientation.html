New Department Chair Orientation

New Department Chair Orientations are held annually or every other year in the Fall Quarter at the office of Academic Personnel to provide pertinent information and resources to newly appointed department chairs. Past events have included topics regarding budget, safety, conflict resolution and employee assistance. The event also includes a panel of experienced chairs who provide valuable advice and tips to incoming chairs.
